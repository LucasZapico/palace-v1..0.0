##### Click Through Rate(CTR)
 
$$ \frac{Clicks}{Impressions} $$

##### Cost per Click

$$\frac{Total Costs}{Total Clicks} $$

##### Conversions Rate 

$$ \frac{Total Conversions}{Total Clicks}$$

##### Cost Per Acquisition 

$$ \frac{Conversions}{Spend} $$
